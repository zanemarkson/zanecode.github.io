Bio Sketch

I was born in Jilin City, a small and quiet town at northeastern China. After spending 18 beautiful years there, in 2006, I started my college life at University of Science and Technology of China, a very unique and charming school. Go ahead and google it. You will find that school very interesting. During those 4 years, I tried my best to learn everything I could and not to waste any time before I graduated with an honored bachelor degree in chemical physics in 2010. I also met my beautiful wife there, which is another blessing I am extremely grateful to.
Right now I am a PhD student in computational chemistry at Duke University. Although chemistry has been the main theme for the majority of my life, I have come to realize that there are other things that I am passionate about: data science and web technology. For the data science, I am proficient on programming in C, Java, Python and other scripting language such as Bash. I am also familiar with high-performance computing and distributive computation frameworks such as Hadoop and Spark. Popular machine learning algorithms are no strangers to me either as I frequently practice on Kaggle. Web technology is another interest of mine, especially the MEAN javascript stack and Websocket (socket.io) technology. And without any surprise, I also look for chance to transform my web applications into desktop/mobile applications using the Github Electron and Ionic.
